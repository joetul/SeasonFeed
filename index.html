How this works

Click RSS on any series to copy its feed URL and paste it into Feedly, NetNewsWire, Inoreader, or any RSS app.

Metadata is generated from TVmaze, and this project intentionally keeps update pacing friendly to public API limits.

Cannot find your series?

Sometimes a show is missing because it is brand new, named differently on TVmaze, or simply not requested yet.

  • Title mismatch: regional titles and punctuation can differ.
  • New or unreleased shows may not be listed yet in source metadata.
  • Community list curation means requests and contributions matter.
